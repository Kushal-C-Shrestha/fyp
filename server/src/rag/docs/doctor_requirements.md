# Doctor Verification Requirements (Nepal)

Doctors must be verified before they can be listed on the platform or accept
appointment bookings.

This document explains what information and documents doctors are expected
to provide during the verification process.

---

## Basic Doctor Information (Required)

Doctors must provide the following personal and professional details:

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Profile photo (optional)
- Professional description or bio

---

## Professional Credentials (Required)

Doctors must submit valid professional information:

- Medical license number
- Total years of professional experience
- Primary medical specialization(s)
- Consultation fee

Doctors may list multiple specializations if applicable.

---

## Qualifications & Experience (Recommended)

Doctors are encouraged to provide structured information about:

### Educational qualifications

Examples:

- MBBS
- MD / MS
- DM / MCh
- Diploma or fellowship programs

### Work experience

Examples:

- Hospital or clinic name
- Role or position
- Start year and end year

Providing detailed qualifications and experience improves profile credibility
and patient trust.

---

## Availability Information (Required to Accept Bookings)

Doctors must provide availability details, such as:

- Days of availability
- Time ranges
- Online or physical consultation (if supported)

Availability may be updated after approval and is used for appointment scheduling.

---

## Verification Documents (Required / Recommended)

Doctors must upload documents to support verification.

Commonly required documents include:

- Medical council registration certificate  
  (e.g., Nepal Medical Council registration)
- Citizenship or official government-issued ID

Optional documents (if applicable):

- Academic certificates
- Experience letters
- Fellowship or specialization certificates

Document requirements may vary depending on specialization and regulations.

---

## Verification Process (Platform Flow)

1. Doctor submits profile details and professional information.
2. Doctor uploads required verification documents.
3. Platform admin reviews the submission:
   - verifies completeness and consistency
   - may request clarification or additional documents
4. Verification result:
   - **Approved**: doctor profile becomes visible and eligible for bookings
   - **Rejected**: doctor is notified with the reason and may re-submit

---

## Professional Conduct & Platform Rules

- Doctors must provide accurate and truthful information.
- Impersonation or false credentials result in rejection.
- Doctors must not offer prescriptions or diagnoses through the platform chat
  without a formal consultation.
- The platform may suspend or re-review doctors if concerns are reported.

---

## Important Notes

- Verification requirements may differ based on medical specialty.
- The platform does not certify legal compliance.
- For legal clarification, doctors should consult the relevant medical authority.
