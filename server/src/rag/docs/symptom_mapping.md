# Symptom to Specialty Mapping (Non-Diagnostic)

This document maps user-reported symptoms to appropriate medical specialties.
The assistant must recommend a specialty, NOT a disease or treatment.

---

## General Rules

- Never diagnose conditions.
- Never prescribe medicines or dosages.
- If symptoms are severe, sudden, or worsening, advise urgent medical attention.
- When unsure, prefer General Physician as the first point of care.
- Ask minimal clarifying questions before mapping.

---

## Common Clarifying Questions

Ask only if needed:

- Age group (child / adult / elderly)
- Duration (hours / days / weeks / months)
- Severity (mild / moderate / severe)
- Sudden onset or gradual?
- Any red flags present?

---

## Red Flag Symptoms (Emergency Guidance)

If ANY of these are mentioned, recommend immediate medical care:

- severe chest pain
- difficulty breathing
- loss of consciousness
- uncontrolled bleeding
- sudden weakness or paralysis
- severe dehydration
- high fever with confusion
- seizure

Do NOT continue normal mapping in these cases.

## Primary Symptom Categories

---

## Fever / Infection-like Symptoms

**Symptoms:**

- fever
- chills
- body ache
- fatigue
- mild cough
- sore throat

**Recommended Specialty:**

- General Physician / Internal Medicine

**Notes:**

- Pediatrics if patient is a child
- Escalate if fever is very high or persistent

---

## Respiratory Symptoms

**Symptoms:**

- cough
- breathing difficulty (non-emergency)
- wheezing
- chest congestion
- asthma symptoms

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Pulmonology
- General Physician for first evaluation

---

## ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)

**Symptoms:**

- ear pain
- hearing loss
- ringing in ears
- sore throat
- tonsil pain
- sinus pain
- nasal blockage
- vertigo related to ear

**Recommended Specialty:**

- ENT (Otolaryngology)

---

## Gastrointestinal / Digestive Issues

**Symptoms:**

- stomach pain
- acidity
- heartburn
- bloating
- nausea
- vomiting
- constipation
- diarrhea
- loss of appetite

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Gastroenterology
- General Physician for mild or early symptoms

---

## Neurological Symptoms

**Symptoms:**

- frequent headaches
- migraines
- dizziness
- vertigo (non-ENT related)
- numbness
- tingling
- memory issues
- tremors (non-emergency)

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Neurology

---

## Cardiovascular Symptoms

**Symptoms:**

- chest discomfort (non-emergency)
- palpitations
- high blood pressure
- irregular heartbeat
- swelling in legs

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Cardiology

**Important:**
If chest pain is severe or associated with sweating or shortness of breath → emergency guidance.

---

## Musculoskeletal / Orthopedic Issues

**Symptoms:**

- joint pain
- back pain
- knee pain
- shoulder pain
- muscle pain
- stiffness
- sports injury
- posture-related pain

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Orthopedics
- Physiotherapy (for strain/posture-related pain)

---

## Skin / Hair / Nail Problems

**Symptoms:**

- skin rash
- itching
- acne
- eczema
- fungal infection
- hair loss
- dandruff
- nail changes

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Dermatology

---

## Mental Health / Emotional Well-Being

**Symptoms:**

- anxiety
- stress
- panic attacks
- mood swings
- sadness
- sleep problems
- lack of motivation

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Psychiatry
- Clinical Psychology (therapy-focused)

**Notes:**

- Do not label conditions
- Encourage professional consultation gently

---

## Eye-Related Symptoms

**Symptoms:**

- eye pain
- redness
- blurry vision
- watering eyes
- light sensitivity
- eye strain

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Ophthalmology

---

## Urinary / Kidney Symptoms

**Symptoms:**

- burning while urinating
- frequent urination
- lower abdominal pain
- blood in urine
- difficulty urinating

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Urology
- Gynecology if female with pelvic symptoms

---

## Reproductive / Women’s Health

**Symptoms:**

- irregular periods
- painful menstruation
- pregnancy-related concerns
- PCOS symptoms
- pelvic pain
- abnormal discharge

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Gynecology / Obstetrics

---

## Dental / Oral Health

**Symptoms:**

- tooth pain
- gum bleeding
- mouth ulcers
- jaw pain
- bad breath

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Dentistry

---

## Pediatric Symptoms (Children)

**Symptoms:**

- fever in children
- poor feeding
- developmental concerns
- frequent infections
- growth issues

**Recommended Specialty:**

- Pediatrics

---

## When to Default to General Physician

Use General Physician when:

- symptoms are vague
- multiple systems are involved
- first-time complaint
- user is unsure where to go

---

## Output Format Recommendation

When responding to users:

- Mention specialty
- Offer next action (check doctors / book appointment)

Example:
"Based on your symptoms, it would be best to consult a Gastroenterologist. I can help you find available doctors near you."
