# Hospital / Clinic Verification Requirements (Nepal)

Hospitals and clinics must be verified before they can be listed on the platform
or accept appointment bookings.

This document explains what information and documents hospitals are expected
to provide during verification.

---

## Basic Hospital Information (Required)

Hospitals or clinics must provide the following details:

- Official hospital or clinic name
- Hospital type
  - Public
  - Private
  - Charity
- Year of establishment
- Hospital description
- Full hospital address
- Map location link (optional)
- Website or social page (optional)

---

## Registration & Legal Information (Required)

Hospitals must submit valid registration-related details:

- Hospital registration number
- License or authority under which the hospital operates  
  (for example: Ministry of Health or relevant local authority)
- Primary official email address
- Primary contact phone number
- Reception phone number

Optional (if available):

- Alternate email address
- Alternate contact phone number

All submitted information must belong to the hospital.

---

## Departments & Services (Recommended)

Hospitals are encouraged to list the medical departments or services they offer.
This helps patients discover the hospital more easily.

Examples:

- General Medicine
- Pediatrics
- Gynecology
- Orthopedics
- ENT
- Dermatology
- Cardiology

---

## Facilities Information (Optional)

Hospitals may list facilities available at their location, such as:

- Emergency services
- ICU
- Diagnostic laboratory
- Pharmacy
- Ambulance service
- Parking
- Wheelchair accessibility

Providing facility details improves patient trust but is not mandatory.

---

## Hospital Administrator Details (Required)

Each hospital must nominate an administrator who will manage the hospital profile
on the platform.

Administrator details include:

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Date of birth
- Gender

The administrator is responsible for:

- managing hospital information
- responding to verification requests
- adding doctors and updating services after approval

---

## Verification Documents (Required / Recommended)

Hospitals are required to upload supporting documents for verification.

Commonly requested documents include:

- Hospital or business registration certificate
- License or permission to operate health services
- Citizenship or official ID of the authorized representative

Optional documents (if applicable):

- PAN/VAT registration document
- Tax clearance document
- Photos of hospital facilities
- Short verification video of the premises

The exact document requirements may vary depending on hospital type and location.

---

## Verification Process (Platform Flow)

1. Hospital submits profile details and administrator information.
2. Hospital uploads required verification documents.
3. Platform admin reviews the submission:
   - checks completeness and consistency
   - may request clarification or additional documents
4. Verification result:
   - **Approved**: hospital becomes visible and eligible for bookings
   - **Rejected**: hospital is notified with a reason and may re-submit

---

## Important Notes

- Verification requirements may differ based on local regulations.
- The platform does not provide legal certification.
- The assistant should not confirm legal compliance.
- For legal clarity, hospitals should consult the relevant government authority.
